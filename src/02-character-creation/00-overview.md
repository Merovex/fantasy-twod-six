# Character Creation

## Character Stats

Heros are defined through six core Stats that shape their path and interactions with the world around them. These stats are:

* Strength determines your physical power, affecting your ability to wield weapons and your performance in combat.
 Agility influences your speed and dexterity, dictating your turn order in battle and your ability to avoid danger.
 Wisdom reflects your understanding of the magical forces that permeate Aetheria, affecting your spellcasting abilities and resistance to magic.
 Charisma is the measure of your persuasive power, crucial for negotiating with friends and foes alike.
 Intellect represents your problem-solving skills, aiding in puzzle-solving and unlocking the secrets of ancient lore.
 Endurance is your stamina and health, determining how much damage you can withstand before falling.
